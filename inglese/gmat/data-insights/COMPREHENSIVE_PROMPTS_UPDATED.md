## 🌍 General Specifications (ALL Types)

### **Language Requirements**
✅ Business formal register (no contractions, no colloquialisms)
✅ Active voice preferred (70% active, 30% passive)
✅ Present tense for current states, past for historical

### **Forbidden Elements**
❌ Personal pronouns (I, you, we)
❌ Hypothetical scenarios ("Suppose that", "Imagine if")
❌ Ambiguous terms (several, many, few, some)
❌ Real company names (use TechCorp, DataSoft, GlobalTech instead)
❌ Cultural/regional specific knowledge
❌ Outdated technology references

### **Context Realism**
✅ Business names: 12 fictional names provided
✅ Dates: Q1-Q4 2023-2024, months, years (all 2020-2025 range)
✅ Currency: USD default, EUR/GBP with realistic exchange rates
✅ Commas in thousands: $1,234.56 not $1234.56

### **Quality Checks (CRITICAL)**
✅ Run calculations 3x to verify
✅ Verify percentages sum to 100% where applicable
✅ Simplify ratios to lowest terms
✅ Only ONE correct answer (never ambiguous)
✅ All distractors clearly wrong
✅ Solvable in 2.0-2.5 minutes
✅ 3-5 steps maximum

### **Common Errors to Avoid**
❌ Same number twice in different contexts
❌ Partial information giving answer away
❌ Technically true but misleading statements
❌ Irrelevant data that doesn't affect answer
❌ Perfect round numbers (50%, 100, etc.)
❌ Requires real-world knowledge beyond given data

### **Final Validation Checklist**
☐ Calculations verified 3 times
☐ Exactly one correct answer
☐ All required JSON fields present
☐ Numbers follow specs (no 10,20,25,50,100)
☐ Word counts within limits
☐ Difficulty matches request
☐ No forbidden elements
☐ Business names fictional
☐ Dates 2020-2025
☐ Language formal
